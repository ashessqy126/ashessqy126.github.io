Qiyang Song

Insitute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences

- Email: songqiyang@iie.ac.cn

I received the B.S. degree in Information Security from University of Electronic and Science Techology of China (UESTC) and received the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Techology from Tsinghua University. Later in 2021, I also worked as a research assistant in George Mason University (GMU), under supervision of Prof. Kun Sun. Now, I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Insitute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My current research interests include Software Security, Web3 Security, and Intrusion Detection System. I welcome colloration and would love to discuss any related academic topics. Feel free to reach out to me via email! I’m eager to communicate with researchers from all over the world.
