About me

I received the B.S. degree in Information Security from University of Electronic and Science Techology of China (UESTC), in 2017. Then, I received the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Techology from Tsinghua University, in 2020. Now, I am a first-year Ph.D. student at George Mason University (GMU), advised by Prof. Kun Sun.

Research

My research interests include

  • Automatic Patching

  • Software Security

  • Cloud Security

  • Machine Learning Security
